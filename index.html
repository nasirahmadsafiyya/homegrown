FARM PRODUCE

Fresh fruits and vegetables are an essential component of farm produce. These can be seasonal or year-round, depending on the region and climate. Common types of produce include apples, oranges, tomatoes, lettuce, carrots, potatoes, and corn. Grains like wheat, rice, barley, and corn are another important category of farm produce. They are used as staple food items globally and are processed into various products such as flour, bread, pasta, and cereals.
